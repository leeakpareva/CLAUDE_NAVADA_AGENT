How Moneytree Will Be Built

Moneytree will be fully built and hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS), the world's leading cloud platform. Here is what the infrastructure looks like:

Service What It Does
AWS Lambda Runs marketing tasks on-demand. You only pay when the agent is working, not when it is idle.
Amazon Bedrock Enterprise AI engine. Powers content writing, trend analysis, audience insights, and brand-voice copywriting.
Amazon S3 + CloudFront Stores all generated images, videos, and content assets. CloudFront delivers them globally at speed.
Amazon RDS / DynamoDB Database for leads, content calendar, analytics, customer interactions, and campaign performance.
Amazon SES Email marketing at scale. Automated sequences, newsletters, and lead nurture campaigns.
AWS Step Functions Orchestrates the full marketing workflow: research, create, publish, monitor, optimise, repeat.
Amazon EventBridge Scheduling engine. Triggers content at optimal times across Pinterest, YouTube, Instagram, Twitter/X.
Amazon QuickSight Visual dashboards showing campaign performance, audience growth, conversion rates, and ROI.
Cloudflare CDN + WAF Security layer. Protects the platform from attacks and ensures fast global content delivery.
Telegram / WhatsApp Control You control Moneytree from your phone. Approve posts, check analytics, trigger campaigns via chat.
