What Your Marketing Agent Does

A 4-pillar autonomous system for digital health product marketing, running 24/7

1

Content Creation

Researches trending health and wellness topics daily. Writes platform-specific copy in your brand voice. Generates Pinterest pins, Twitter threads, YouTube descriptions, Meta posts. Creates visual assets using AI image generation. Maintains a content calendar that runs itself.

2

Lead Generation

Identifies potential customers from engagement signals (comments, follows, DMs). Verifies contact details. Sends introductory outreach in your brand voice. Manages follow-up sequences automatically. Monitors your inbox for responses.

3

Strategic Monitoring

Tracks engagement, reach, and conversions across all platforms. Monitors competitor content and market trends. Sends daily intelligence reports. Identifies which product topics generate the most interest. Adapts strategy based on data.

4

Command and Control

Controlled entirely from your phone via Telegram. Send a message like "post about my new skincare guide" and the agent handles everything. Approve posts before they go live, or let it run autonomously. Check stats anytime. Switch between fast and deep-reasoning AI.
