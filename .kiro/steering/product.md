# NAVADA Edge — Product Overview

NAVADA Edge is a distributed AI infrastructure platform built by Lee Akpareva (Founder, Principal AI Consultant). It powers 24/7 autonomous operations: Telegram bot (Chief of Staff), automated outreach, lead generation, trading, monitoring, and AI vision.

## Users
- **Lee Akpareva** — Founder, sole operator. Commands the network via Telegram, Claude Code, and Kiro.
- **Demo guests** — Restricted Telegram access for NAVADA Edge demos.

## Core Value
Zero-terminal, always-on AI operations. Claude acts as Chief of Staff with full system control. Kiro agents extend capabilities with specialised AI workflows.

## Business Objectives
- Autonomous AI consultancy operations (email, outreach, reporting)
- 24/7 infrastructure monitoring and self-healing
- AI-powered lead generation and prospect pipeline
- Real-time vision AI (face detection, YOLO, analysis)
- Multi-channel comms: Telegram, SMS, voice, email, LinkedIn
